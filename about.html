OUR BUSINESS

At the heart of our operations lies an innovative on-demand production model. With this model, we are able to provide a seamless and instant direct-to-consumer experience, while minimising waste.

We are committed to creating a more sustainable future for our company and the planet, and so in addition to our production model; we carefully select our fabrics and materials to ensure our garments are long-lasting. We also engage in small batch sourcing to ensure quality and avoid overproduction. In addition, leftover fabrics are creatively repurposed to produce functional lifestyle products.

Over the years, we have been able to reach customers in over 30 countries with our worldwide shipping. Our global reach allows us to connect with women from diverse backgrounds and cultures, creating a truly inclusive and international Classic ware community.
